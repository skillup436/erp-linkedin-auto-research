# Example EN 2 — High Performing Post (ERP Consulting Framework)

## Metadata
- Language: English
- Hook Type: Bold statement with numbers
- Content Type: Framework / Reality check
- Angle: Data-driven + contrarian
- Tone: Direct, no-nonsense
- Estimated Impressions: Very High

## Post Text

ERP consulting is 20% technical expertise, 30% problem-solving, and 50% expectation management.

A consultant's job isn't just about knowing the system—it's about:

Understanding business challenges.
Finding practical solutions.
Aligning stakeholders with reality.

Most ERP projects don't fail due to bad software; they fail due to misaligned expectations.

Have you seen an ERP project struggle because what was promised didn't match what was delivered?

## Why This Works
- Opens with a bold mathematical breakdown (grabs attention)
- Challenges the assumption that ERP is technical (contrarian)
- Three-item list is scannable and memorable
- The punchline (misaligned expectations) resonates deeply with practitioners
- Question invites war stories in comments — drives high comment engagement
