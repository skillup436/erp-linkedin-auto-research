## Current Hypothesis

Based on analysis of high-performing ERP consulting posts, the strongest content combines:
1. **Transformation hooks** with specific numbers ("J'ai fait 47 projets ERP. Voici ce que personne ne dit.")
2. **Contrarian observations** that challenge mainstream consulting advice
3. **Short, punchy format** — 2-3 short paragraphs max, never a wall of text
4. **A provocative question** at the end to drive comments

### English

## Top Performing Content Types (ranked)

1. **Harsh truths** — uncomfortable realities about ERP/consulting that resonate
2. **Expectation vs. reality** — what clients expect vs. what actually happens
3. **Contrarian takes** — challenging popular advice or trendy buzzwords
4. **Lessons from experience** — specific stories/observations from 25+ years
5. **Change management insights** — the human side of ERP (stakeholders, resistance)

## Format Guidelines

- **Length**: 120-250 words sweet spot
- **Line structure**: Max 2-3 sentences per paragraph
- **White space**: Lots of it — one idea per line for easy scanning
- **Lists**: Use sparingly (2-4 items max) when listing specific points
- **Emojis**: None or max 1-2 — keep it professional
- **Hashtags**: Max 3, highly relevant only
